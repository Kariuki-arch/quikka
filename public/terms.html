2. Description of Service

Quikka allows independent merchants to generate custom M-Pesa payment links and manage inventory via WhatsApp. All transactions are processed securely through our licensed third-party payment partner, IntaSend.

3. Fees and Settlements

  • For Buyers: Using a Quikka checkout link is completely free. You only pay the standard Safaricom M-Pesa transfer charges applicable to your transaction.
  • For Merchants: Quikka charges a flat software fee of KES 15 per successful transaction. There are no setup fees or monthly subscription costs.
  • Settlements: Merchant funds are held securely throughout the day and are automatically disbursed, minus our KES 15 fee, to the merchant's registered personal M-Pesa account every evening at 6:00 PM EAT.

  • Quikka Fees: If a merchant issues a refund to a buyer, the KES 15 transaction fee originally collected by Quikka is non-refundable.
